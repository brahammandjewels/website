Our Story

Three Generations of
Getting It Right

It started in 1965, when my grandfather opened a small retail jewellery shop in Mumbai. Three decades later, in 1994, my father took the business further — setting up our own casting factory, piecing it together with second-hand machines and a lot of grit.

That factory is where Brahammand Jewels as you know it today was really born — we spotted a gap for genuinely well-finished, exclusive CZ jewellery, at a price that made sense for jewellers across the country. Today, I run the business my grandfather and father built, still guided by the same idea: earn every reorder. That same instinct is why we're now also expanding into 9kt gold — an emerging market we're seeing real demand for, and building our design range around it.
